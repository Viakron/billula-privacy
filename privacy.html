Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Overview

Billula ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our expense tracking application.

Privacy First: Billula is designed with privacy at its core. All your financial data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us:

Data Type Purpose Storage
Expense records Track your spending Local Only
Categories & budgets Organize expenses Local Only
Receipt photos Keep records Local Only
App preferences Personalization Local Only
Scheduled bills Bill reminders Local Only

Data Processed by Third-Party Services

When you use the receipt scanning feature, the following occurs:

Data Type Service Purpose
Receipt images Google Cloud Vision OCR Processing

Receipt Scanning: When you scan a receipt, the image is sent to Google Cloud Vision API for text extraction. Google processes the image to extract text and returns the results. Google's use of this data is governed by their Cloud Privacy Notice.

How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to your personal financial information. The data on your device is used solely to:

  • Display your expense history and analytics
  • Calculate budgets and spending summaries
  • Send local notifications for bill reminders
  • Generate expense reports for export

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • Local Storage: All financial data remains on your device
  • Biometric Authentication: Optional Face ID/Touch ID protection
  • No Account Required: No personal information needed to use the app
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data is not synced to any cloud servers

Your Responsibility: Since data is stored locally, we recommend regularly backing up your device and enabling the app's backup feature to prevent data loss.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. The only exception is:

  • Receipt Scanning: When you choose to scan a receipt, the image is processed by Google Cloud Vision API to extract text. This is an optional feature that you initiate.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app at any time
  • Export: Download your data as CSV files
  • Delete: Remove individual expenses or clear all data
  • Backup: Create and restore local backups

To delete all app data, you can uninstall the app or use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings.

Children's Privacy

Billula is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
